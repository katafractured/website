SafeOpen — Privacy Policy

SafeOpen Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2026 — About SafeOpen →

The Short Version

SafeOpen has two tiers with different privacy profiles. The free tier is entirely on-device — no network calls, no data collected. The Pro tier routes link inspection through Katafract’s backend proxy so your real IP is never exposed to the destination, and uses AI to summarize what a link contains. This policy explains exactly what happens in each case.

Free Tier — On-Device Only

Free users of SafeOpen get QR scanning, URL decoding, payload classification, and risk scoring. Every part of this runs locally on your device:

  • QR codes and their decoded content never leave your device
  • Links you paste or share into the app are processed locally
  • Risk scoring uses deterministic local algorithms — no network connection required
  • Scan history is stored in local device storage only (not synced to iCloud or any cloud service)
  • Camera frames are processed in memory and are never written to disk or transmitted

For free-tier users, Katafract receives no data whatsoever.

Pro Tier — Backend Processing

SafeOpen Pro adds AI-powered link analysis and the ability to open links in a disposable browser session with a unique IPv6 identity. These features require backend infrastructure. Here is what happens when you use them:

Link Inspection (AI Analysis)

When you tap “Analyze & Open Safely,” the URL you are inspecting is sent to Katafract’s backend. Our servers fetch the destination page anonymously using our proxy infrastructure, then send the page content to OpenAI’s API to generate a plain-English summary of what the link contains. The summary is returned to your device and displayed in the app.

  • What Katafract receives: The URL being inspected, your anonymous device ID (see below), and a request timestamp. We do not log your real IP address in association with inspection requests.
  • What OpenAI receives: The content of the fetched page (not your personal information, not your device ID, not your IP). OpenAI processes this under their API terms and privacy policy. We use OpenAI solely for content summarization.
  • Retention: Inspection session data is ephemeral. Session records are automatically deleted after 10 minutes. We do not retain a history of URLs you have inspected.

Open Safely (Disposable IPv6 Session)

When you open a link using SafeOpen Pro, the connection is routed through a disposable /128 IPv6 address assigned exclusively for that session. The destination website sees this datacenter IPv6 address — never your real device IP. The IPv6 address is revoked and discarded when you close the session or after 10 minutes, whichever comes first.

Anonymous Device ID

SafeOpen Pro generates a random UUID on first launch and stores it in your device’s secure Keychain. This identifier is sent with backend requests solely for rate limiting and session management. It is not tied to your Apple ID, name, email, or any personal information. It cannot be used to identify you. You can reset it by deleting and reinstalling the app.

Camera Permission

SafeOpen requests camera access solely to scan QR codes in real time. It does not access your photo library, save images, or use the camera for any other purpose. You may deny camera permission and use the paste workflow instead.

Subscription & Payments

SafeOpen Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. Katafract does not receive your Apple ID, payment method, billing address, or any financial information. To manage or cancel your subscription, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device, or visit apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI — Used by Pro tier for link content summarization. Page content (not personal data) is transmitted. Subject to OpenAI’s Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — Handles all subscription billing. Subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy.

SafeOpen integrates no advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks, or crash-reporting services.

Apple Privacy Nutrition Label

The App Store Privacy Nutrition Label for SafeOpen declares:

  • Free tier: Data Not Collected
  • Pro tier: Device ID (anonymous UUID, used for App Functionality, Not Linked to You)

Children

SafeOpen is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be announced at katafract.com and reflected in an updated effective date above. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
