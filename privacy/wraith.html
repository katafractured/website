Enclave VPN — Privacy Policy

Enclave VPN Privacy Policy

Katafract LLC — Last updated April 2026 — All app policies →

Plain English: Enclave VPN does not log your traffic, DNS queries, browsing history, connection times, or real IP address. We collect only what is needed to operate your account: email, subscription tier, device label, and assigned tunnel IP. Node health data (CPU, memory, disk) is about our servers — not your activity.

Strict No-Log Policy

We do not inspect, log, cache, or store any content passing through our VPN tunnels. DNS queries are handled on-node by AdGuard Home (Haven DNS) and are never persisted to disk or sent to our control plane. Your real IP is never recorded — external services see only the shared exit IP of the Enclave node you connected to. WireGuard handshakes, session start/end times, and connection metadata are not logged.

WireGuard Protocol and Session Keys

Enclave VPN uses WireGuard with Curve25519 key exchange, ChaCha20 encryption, and Poly1305 authentication. Session keys are ephemeral — generated fresh per session and discarded on disconnect. Keys are never logged or reused. Packet metadata is processed in kernel memory and never written to disk.

Haven DNS Protection

Haven DNS (powered by AdGuard Home) runs on every Enclave node. DNS queries are resolved locally on the node and are never forwarded to our control plane. We do not log, store, or analyze individual DNS queries. Aggregate blocklist statistics (cluster-wide, not per-user) may be collected for capacity planning only. Blocklists: AdGuard DNS Filter + OISD Full.

Node Health Diagnostics

Enclave nodes send a heartbeat to our control plane every 30 seconds containing: CPU %, memory %, disk %, and aggregate fail2ban event count. This is infrastructure data about our servers, not about user activity. It does not include active user counts, per-user bandwidth, connection logs, or any user-identifying information. Node health data is retained in a rolling 90-day window, then purged.

Account and Identity

Accounts are managed by Zitadel (our Sigil identity platform). We store your email, hashed password (bcrypt, never recoverable), subscription tier, account creation timestamp, and device records. We do not store your real IP, connection history, or payment method details.

Payments and Billing

Subscriptions are processed by Apple (iOS/macOS), Google (Android), or Stripe (web). We receive only subscription status and renewal dates — never credit card numbers, CVV, or full billing details. Subscription records are retained for 7 years as required by US tax law.

Data Retention

  • Account data: retained until account deletion, purged within 30 days of request
  • Device peer records: deleted immediately when device is removed
  • Payment records: retained 7 years per US tax law
  • Node health data: 90-day rolling window, then auto-purged
  • Traffic and connection logs: not generated — nothing to retain

International Data Transfers and GDPR

Katafract LLC is a US entity. Account data is processed on US servers. Enclave nodes operate in Germany, Finland, Singapore, and the United States. EU users may select EU exit nodes to minimize data residency outside the EEA.

EU users have the right to access, erasure, portability, rectification, and objection under GDPR. Legal basis: contract (Article 6(1)(b)) for account data; legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)) for node health data; legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)) for payment records. To exercise your rights, contact privacy@katafract.com.

Children’s Privacy

Enclave VPN is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact privacy@katafract.com to remove any child account.

Material changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date. We will email you if changes materially expand data collection or weaken privacy protections.
