3. Description of the Service

WraithVPN provides three tiers of privacy protection:

  • Haven (free and paid tiers) — encrypted DNS (DoH) that blocks ads, trackers, and malicious domains before they reach your device.
  • Enclave — Haven DNS plus a single-hop WireGuard VPN routing your traffic through a Katafract-operated server in a country you select.
  • Enclave Plus — Haven DNS plus a multi-hop WireGuard VPN routing your traffic through two Katafract-operated servers in different jurisdictions.

The Service is provided on a best-effort basis. Server availability, throughput, and latency depend on factors beyond our control, including your Internet connection, the route between your device and our servers, and the policies of the networks you traverse.
