How it's built

Enclave is not a resold product. We run our own infrastructure, write our own software, and operate our own exit nodes. Every component is purpose-built for privacy.

🌐
Haven DNS
AdGuard Home on each node, binding only on WireGuard interface. No public DNS exposure.
🔒
WraithGate Nodes
WireGuard exit nodes in Nuremberg, Helsinki, Singapore, Tokyo, Mumbai, Ashburn, Portland, and Newark. nftables firewall, minimal attack surface.
🕸️
ShroudNet Mesh
Private Headscale mesh connecting all nodes. Management traffic never touches the public internet.
🛡️
Artemis Control
Internal control plane for node health, provisioning, and key rotation. Not publicly accessible.

Privacy commitments

What we don't keep

Not just policy — architecture. We can't produce records we don't hold.

No connection logs

We do not record when you connect, disconnect, or which server you connect to. Session metadata is discarded at session end.

No traffic logs

We do not inspect, log, or retain any information about the sites you visit or the data you transmit through the VPN.

No DNS query logs

Haven DNS resolvers do not log DNS queries or associate queries with IP addresses. No query history is retained.

No accounts

Enclave on iOS uses device-based entitlement verification. No email address or username is required to use the service.

Our warrant canary is updated quarterly to confirm we have not received any secret government orders.
