Questions

Do you log my connections or DNS queries?
No. We store no connection logs, no DNS query logs, no IP addresses. The token you use to authenticate is stored as a one-way hash — we cannot reverse it to identify you. By design, we have nothing to hand over.
What happens if I reinstall the app or lose my token?
For Stripe subscribers, use the recovery flow at connect.katafract.com — enter your subscription ID and we'll send a recovery link to your receipt email. For App Store purchases, your token is tied to your Apple transaction ID and can be re-issued automatically on reinstall.
How do I use this on Windows or Linux?
Visit connect.katafract.com, paste your subscription token, and download a WireGuard .conf file or scan the QR code. Import it into the WireGuard app (available on all platforms). No extra software needed.
What payment methods do you accept?
Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and SEPA/BACS direct debit for EU and UK customers. In-app purchases via the App Store or Google Play use their respective payment systems. Crypto payment (Monero, Lightning) is available for maximum privacy.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel through the customer portal, the App Store, or by emailing support@katafract.com. Your access continues until the end of the billing period. There's a 7-day grace period on failed payments before cancellation.
Where are the VPN nodes?
Currently: Nuremberg (Germany), Helsinki (Finland), Singapore, Tokyo (Japan), Mumbai (India), Ashburn (US East), Portland (US West), and Newark (US East-2). Additional regions are added as the network grows. The Enclave app shows real-time latency so you always connect to the fastest node for your location.
