Getting Started

Vaultyx is a zero-knowledge encrypted cloud storage app built for the Sovereign tier. Every file you upload is encrypted on your device before leaving it — the encryption happens locally, and only you hold the encryption key. Katafract never sees your files or your encryption keys. Files are stored on encrypted S3-compatible object storage and synced automatically across all your devices.
  1. Download Vaultyx from the App Store.
  2. Sign in with Apple. No email or password required.
  3. Subscribe to Sovereign tier ($18/mo or $144/yr) from the app settings.
  4. Create a new vault or unlock an existing one.
  5. Tap the + button and select Upload, then choose files from your device or camera.
Sovereign — $18/mo ($144/yr): Everything in Enclave plus 1 TB of encrypted cloud storage in Vaultyx, cross-device sync, selective sync, folder organization, and all future Katafract apps included.

Storage add-ons — $4/mo per TB: Need more than 1 TB? Purchase additional storage in 1 TB increments at $4 per month.
Vaultyx is available on iOS 17.0 or later on iPhone and iPad. A macOS app is in development. Android is planned. All your Sovereign subscriptions sync automatically across all devices you sign into with the same Apple ID.

Uploading & Managing Files

Open the app, navigate to the vault or folder where you want the file to go, and tap the + button. Select Upload to choose files from your Photos, Files, or any connected app. Vaultyx encrypts the file locally on your device, then uploads the encrypted data. Once complete, you’ll see the file in your vault with a timestamp.
Yes. Vaultyx supports any file type and size up to your remaining storage quota. Large files (over 100 MB) upload in the background, and you can continue using the app while the upload completes. If your app is closed, the upload resumes automatically when you reopen Vaultyx.
In your vault view, tap the folder icon or the + button and select New Folder. Folders are also encrypted and fully synced. You can nest folders inside folders, and each folder is treated as its own encrypted container.
Yes. In the file detail view, add categories to organize and filter files. Categories are synced across your devices and make it easy to find files later. You can apply one or more categories to any file.
Long-press a file to open the actions menu, then tap Rename. To move a file to a different folder, tap and hold the file, then select Move. Choose the destination folder and confirm. Both operations are instant and sync to all your devices.
Files you delete are moved to the Trash and remain recoverable for 30 days. Open the Vault menu and tap Trash, then select the file and tap Restore. After 30 days, files are permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. Deleted files count against your storage quota until they are emptied from Trash.

Offline Access & Syncing

Yes. Vaultyx automatically downloads a synced copy of your files to your device for offline access. You can open, view, and work with offline files without an internet connection. Changes you make offline are uploaded automatically when you reconnect.
By default, Vaultyx syncs all your files to every device. To save space on your iPhone, enable Selective Sync in Settings. Choose which folders or files should download and cache locally. Files not synced locally can still be browsed and accessed through the app, but will require internet to download and view.
Files sync instantly under good network conditions. If you upload a file on your iPhone, it will be available on your iPad or Mac within seconds. If you’re offline, changes sync as soon as you reconnect. You can also manually trigger a sync by pulling down in the file list.

Encryption & Security

Zero-knowledge means Katafract has no ability to see your files or decrypt your data. Your encryption key is generated and stored only on your devices — it never leaves your control. Files are encrypted before they leave your device and remain encrypted in storage. Only you, and anyone you explicitly share files with, can decrypt and read your vault.
This is critical: If you forget your encryption key, your vault cannot be recovered. Katafract cannot decrypt your files or help you regain access. The encryption key is derived from your device’s local keychain and is not stored anywhere else. If you lose access to all devices where you signed in, your vault is permanently locked. Back up your key in a safe place, or write down the recovery code offered during setup and store it securely offline.
Your vault is protected by your device’s security and by Vaultyx’s encryption. An attacker with your unlocked device would need your encryption key to read your files. If your device is locked, they would need your passcode to even access the Vaultyx app. We recommend enabling biometric (Face ID / Touch ID) authentication in Vaultyx Settings for an additional layer.
We can only hand over what exists in a decrypted form. Vaultyx is designed so that Katafract never holds your encryption keys and never stores plaintext versions of your files. Even if we received a legal order to produce your data, we would have nothing to produce. The encrypted files in storage cannot be decrypted without your key.

Storage & Billing

Sovereign includes 1 TB (1,000 GB) of encrypted cloud storage. You can view your usage at any time in app Settings. Deleted files in Trash count against your quota; empty Trash to reclaim space.
When you approach your storage limit, the app will prompt you to purchase an add-on. Each add-on grants an additional 1 TB and costs $4/mo. You can purchase multiple add-ons to get 2 TB, 3 TB, or more. Add-ons are billed monthly alongside your Sovereign subscription.
Subscriptions purchased through the App Store are managed by Apple. Go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions to upgrade your plan, add storage add-ons, or cancel. Cancellation stops renewal at the end of the current billing period.
Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com. Katafract cannot issue refunds for in-app purchases. If you have a billing dispute, contact Apple Support directly.

Cross-Device Sync & App Entitlements

Yes. Sign in to Vaultyx on any device with your Apple ID. Your Sovereign subscription and vault are automatically recognized on all signed-in devices. Files and folders sync seamlessly across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
Sign in to Vaultyx with the same Apple ID used on your original device. Tap Restore Purchases in Settings. Your subscription will be recognized instantly, and your vault will begin syncing. No re-payment is required.
All Katafract Sovereign apps (Vaultyx, WraithVPN, and others) share a single subscription via the iOS App Group group.com.katafract.enclave. If you subscribe to Sovereign in one app, all other Katafract apps automatically recognize your subscription without duplicate purchases.
Android support is planned for a future release. For now, Vaultyx is available on iOS 17.0 or later. You can pre-register your interest on the Vaultyx app page.

Troubleshooting

Try these steps in order:
  1. Check that you have an active Sovereign subscription in Settings.
  2. Ensure you have a stable internet connection (WiFi or cellular).
  3. Force-quit Vaultyx and reopen it.
  4. Check your available storage quota. If full, delete files or purchase an add-on.
  5. Try uploading a smaller file first to test.
  6. If still blocked, restart your device and try again.
Still not working? Email us with your iOS version and device model.
Tap Restore Purchases in Settings to force a subscription check. If the issue persists after 60 seconds, sign out and back in with Apple. The app validates subscriptions against Apple’s servers — a brief network hiccup can cause a stale status to display.

You can view sync progress in the app settings under “Sync Status.” Large files over 500 MB may take longer depending on your connection speed.
If deleted less than 30 days ago, open the Vault menu and tap Trash. Find the file, select it, and tap Restore. Once restored, the file returns to its original folder and resumes syncing to all your devices.
