Philosophy

The cataphract never
forgot who they were

In the ancient world, the cataphract was the most heavily armored warrior on the battlefield — rider and horse sheathed in layered steel. But unlike infantry ground into interchangeable units, every cataphract fought as an individual. Their own mount, their own lance, their own identity beneath the armor.

We build software the same way. Every app solves a real problem we've experienced personally. Every app runs entirely on your device. Every app is priced once and fairly — no subscriptions unless we're paying for compute on your behalf.

Maximum protection. Zero loss of self. Your tools should serve you, not shape you.
