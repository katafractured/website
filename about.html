About Katafract

Independent software, built for the long game

Katafract LLC is an independent software studio building privacy-first software. Our iPhone apps solve real problems and stay on your device. Our Enclave platform — Enclave VPN, Haven DNS, and Sigil — protects everything you do online. One conviction runs through all of it: your data is yours.

A cataphract was the heaviest cavalry in the ancient world — fully armored, unstoppable. We build apps that give your data the same protection.

Your phone already holds your most sensitive information: your location, your photos, your identity documents. We build around that fact — not by moving data to servers we control, but by keeping it on the device that already belongs to you.

Enclave Platform

Beyond the apps, we operate Enclave — a privacy platform for people who want to own their network, identity, and digital perimeter. Haven provides encrypted DNS that blocks ads and trackers. Enclave VPN routes your traffic through global nodes with no logs and no identity required. Vault provides zero-knowledge encrypted cloud storage. Sigil issues token-based identities so you never have to hand over an email address.

Infrastructure

Katafract's platform is built on a global distributed S3 storage cluster and WireGuard mesh. Every file in Vault is encrypted client-side before it ever touches our servers. The cluster replicates each object 3 times across geographic zones (US East, US West, EU, Asia) to ensure durability and low latency. Bandwidth is unmetered and uncapped — 500Mbps+ per node. Standard S3 API means you own your data: export it anytime with any S3 client, no lock-in. The entire platform runs on WireGuard mesh networking, with zero-knowledge design — we encrypt everything we can and store only what we must.

How We Build

Our core principle: your data is never collected, retained, or sold. For ExifArmor, ParkArmor, and DocArmor, all processing happens entirely on your device — no servers, no network calls, nothing to breach. SafeOpen is different by necessity: inspecting a link requires fetching it, so we do that fetch through our own privacy proxy. We see the destination so you don't have to expose your device to it. We log nothing about you.

Our apps are free to try and fair to upgrade. ExifArmor and ParkArmor are free with core functionality — no accounts, no timers, no expiry. A single $3.99 one-time purchase unlocks the full experience permanently, tied to your Apple ID. SafeOpen is free — 10 scan credits included, refilled monthly. Additional credits available as one-time in-app purchases (100 for $0.99, 500 for $2.99, 2,500 for $9.99). No subscription, no dark patterns, no dark consent flows.
