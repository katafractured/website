About Katafract

Independent software, built for the long game

Katafract LLC is an independent software studio building privacy-first tools for iPhone. We make apps that solve real problems and stay entirely on your device — no accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

A cataphract was the heaviest cavalry in the ancient world — fully armored, unstoppable. We build apps that give your data the same protection.

Your phone already holds your most sensitive information: your location, your photos, your identity documents. We build around that fact — not by moving data to servers we control, but by keeping it on the device that already belongs to you.

How We Build

Every Katafract app ships with the same architecture: all processing happens on your device, no network calls are made for core functionality, and there is no server-side component that could be breached, subpoenaed, or shut down.

Our apps are free to try and fair to upgrade. ExifArmor and ParkArmor are free with core functionality — no accounts, no timers, no expiry. A single $3.99 one-time purchase unlocks the full experience permanently, tied to your Apple ID. DocArmor is $3.99 outright with no tiers at all. No subscriptions, ever.
