Features

Storage built around privacy

Zero-knowledge by design. We cannot read your files, even if we wanted to.

🔐
AES-256-GCM Encryption
Military-grade encryption on your device. Your encryption key never leaves your possession. Katafract stores only encrypted blobs.
⚡
FastCDC Delta Sync
Edit 1 byte of a 1GB file? Only ~64KB uploads. Content-defined chunking means you only sync what changed, not the entire file.
🔄
Auto VPN Routing
All traffic routes through WraithVPN automatically. Your ISP sees the WireGuard tunnel, not your files or metadata.
📸
Photo Backup
Backup original-quality photos and videos. Automatic deduplication by content hash — store the same photo once, even across devices.
📦
Standard S3 Backend
Your data lives in standard S3. Export anytime with any S3 client. No lock-in, no proprietary APIs — just open standards.
🌍
Distributed Globally
Data replicated across US, EU, and Asia. 500Mbps+ unmetered bandwidth per node. Automatic failover, zero manual intervention.

🔒 Mathematically impossible to scan

Vaultyx uses client-side encryption. The server never receives your unencrypted data, never holds your encryption keys, and has no way to read your files. Content scanning, AI training, warrant compliance — all mathematically impossible without your key.

Unlike iCloud: Apple scans photos before they upload. We never see anything to scan. Unlike Dropbox: We have no plaintext copy on servers. Unlike Google Drive: No content analysis, no AI training, no account profiles built from your data.
