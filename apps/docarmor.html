Features

The vault your documents deserve

Built for documents you can’t afford to lose — or expose.

📷
Built-In Scanner
Scan documents directly in-app. VisionKit processes the camera feed on-device. Scans are encrypted before they ever touch disk.
🔐
AES-256 Encryption
Every document is encrypted with AES-256 via Apple CryptoKit. The encryption key lives in the iOS Keychain and never leaves your device.
🫣
Face ID / Touch ID
Your vault locks when you leave the app and requires biometric authentication to reopen. DocArmor never sees your biometric data — only iOS does.
☁️⃠
No iCloud Sync
iCloud sync is explicitly disabled for all vault data. Your documents do not appear in iCloud Drive, iCloud Backup, or any cloud service.
📂
Organized Folders
Create categories like Medical, Travel, or Finance. Every folder uses the same vault key. Everything stays encrypted.
📤
Secure Export
Share a document to Files, AirDrop, or another app when you need it. Export is always explicit — nothing leaves without your action.

🔒 End-to-end local encryption

DocArmor stores your documents encrypted with AES-256. The key is in the iOS Keychain, bound to your device and biometrics. Katafract has no server-side copy of anything — there are no servers at all.
